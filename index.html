Our Story

Serving Western Canada with Pride

Founded in 2019, Green Light Automotive began with a simple mission: to revolutionize the car buying and selling experience across Western Canada. What started as a small operation in Vancouver has grown into Western Canada's most trusted automotive service, with dedicated teams serving British Columbia, Alberta, and Saskatchewan.

Vancouver

Our flagship Vancouver location serves the entire Lower Mainland, from Richmond to North Vancouver. With the largest inventory in BC, we guarantee to find your dream car.

Victoria

Our Victoria team serves the capital region with same-day approvals and island-wide delivery. From James Bay to Oak Bay, we've helped over 5,000 families find their perfect vehicle.

Kelowna

In the heart of the Okanagan, our Kelowna branch specializes in vehicles perfect for both city driving and mountain adventures. We understand the unique needs of interior BC drivers.

Edmonton

Serving Alberta's capital and northern region, our Edmonton team knows trucks and SUVs. From Fort McMurray workers to downtown professionals, we have financing solutions for everyone.

Regina

Our Regina location serves all of southern Saskatchewan with prairie-friendly vehicles. We specialize in reliable winter-ready cars and work trucks for Saskatchewan's hardworking families.

Saskatoon

From the University of Saskatchewan to the industrial areas, our Saskatoon team provides fast approvals and competitive rates. We're proud to serve the Bridge City and surrounding communities.
